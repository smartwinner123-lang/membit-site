Frequently asked questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

  • iOS (App Store): Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Membit
  • Android (Google Play): Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription access, but billing/cancellation is done through Apple/Google.

I paid but premium isn’t unlocked.

  • Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple/Google account you used to purchase.
  • Try closing and reopening the app.
  • If the app has a “Restore Purchases” button, tap it.
  • If it still doesn’t work, email us with a screenshot of your subscription status page.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email membit.help@gmail.com from the email associated with your account with the subject: “Delete my Membit account”. We will verify your request and delete your account and associated app data where applicable.

How is my data stored?

We use Supabase to store account and app data (e.g., progress). See our Privacy Policy for details.
