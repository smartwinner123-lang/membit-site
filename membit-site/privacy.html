Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-01

This Privacy Policy explains how Membit (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile app and related services (the “Service”).

1) Who controls your data

The data controller is the Membit developer/operator. If you have questions or requests, contact: support@membit.app.

2) What we collect

  • Account information: when you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive an identifier and basic profile information (e.g., name/email if you choose to share it).
  • App data you create: vocabulary lists, learning progress, streaks, settings, and content you enter in the app.
  • Device & diagnostics: app version, device type/OS, crash logs, and performance data to keep the app reliable and fix bugs.
  • Purchase status: whether you have an active subscription/premium access. We do not store your full payment card details.

3) How we use information

  • Provide and operate the Service (login, syncing, saving your progress).
  • Personalize learning and improve features.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Manage subscriptions/premium access and restore purchases.

4) Third parties we use

  • Supabase (database/auth/hosting of user data): used to store your account and app content securely.
  • Sign in with Apple and Google Sign-In: used for authentication.
  • RevenueCat: used to manage subscriptions, entitlement status, and purchase receipts across App Store / Google Play.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: payments are processed by the store; we receive subscription status/receipt information via RevenueCat and/or the store APIs.

5) How we share information

We share information only when needed to run the Service:

  • With vendors/partners listed above to provide authentication, storage, and subscription management.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect rights/safety.
  • In connection with a business transfer (e.g., acquisition), with appropriate safeguards.

6) Data retention

We keep your data as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You can request deletion (see below).

7) Your choices and rights

  • Access / update: you can manage account information in the app (where available).
  • Delete: request deletion by emailing support@membit.app.
  • Subscriptions: manage/ cancel via your App Store / Google Play account settings.

8) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9) Children

If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child provided personal information without permission, contact us to request deletion.

10) Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above.

Contact: support@membit.app
