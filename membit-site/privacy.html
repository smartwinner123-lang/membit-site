Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

Membit (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information we collect

  • Account information (optional): email/username if you create an account.
  • App usage data: learning progress (e.g., words studied, review history), app interactions, and basic diagnostics.
  • Device information: device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Analytics data (if enabled): approximate usage patterns to improve the app.

2) How we use information

  • Provide the core learning experience (saving progress, syncing across devices).
  • Improve performance, fix bugs, and develop features.
  • Provide customer support when you contact us.

3) Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help us operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics), under contracts that require protection of your data.

4) Data retention

We keep your data only as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal requirements. You can request deletion.

5) Your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • You can opt out of analytics where available in your device/app settings.

6) Children’s privacy

Membit is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

7) Contact

For privacy requests, contact: support@membit.app (replace with your email)
