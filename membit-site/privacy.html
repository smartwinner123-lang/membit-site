Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-01

This Privacy Policy explains how Membit (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Membit mobile app and related services (the “Service”).

1) Who controls your data

The data controller is the Membit developer/operator. If you have questions or requests, contact: support@membit.app.

2) What we collect

  • Account information: when you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive an identifier for your account and basic profile info (such as name/email if you choose to share it).
  • App data you create: vocabulary lists, learning progress, streaks, settings, and other content you enter in the app.
  • Device & diagnostics: app version, device type/OS, crash logs, and performance data to keep the app reliable and fix bugs.
  • Purchase status: whether you have an active subscription or premium access (we do not store your full payment card details if purchases are handled by the App Store / Play Store).

3) How we use your data

  • Provide core features (sign-in, saving progress, syncing across devices).
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud/abuse, and protect accounts.
  • Improve the Service (debugging, performance, and feature improvements).
  • Customer support (responding to your requests).

4) Authentication (Sign in with Apple / Google)

You can sign in using Apple or Google. These providers authenticate you and may share limited information with us based on your settings with them. We use this to create/verify your Membit account and keep it secure.

5) Our service providers (Supabase)

We use Supabase to store and sync your account and app data (for example, progress and settings). Supabase acts as a processor on our behalf. We only use providers that are necessary to run the Service.

6) Payments & subscriptions (RevenueCat)

Membit uses RevenueCat to help manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. Purchases are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play (not by us). We receive subscription status information (for example: active/inactive, product tier, and renewal state) so we can unlock Premium features and provide customer support. We do not receive your full payment card information from Apple or Google.

7) Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only:

  • With service providers that help operate the app (like hosting/storage) under contract.
  • If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • In a business transfer (merger/acquisition), with appropriate safeguards.

8) Data retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. We may retain limited data for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

9) Your choices & rights

  • Access / correction: you can review or update certain information in the app.
  • Delete your account: request deletion by emailing support@membit.app.
  • Data export: request a copy of your data by emailing us.

10) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

11) Children

Membit is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

12) Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date and, when appropriate, provide notice in the app or on this page.

Contact: support@membit.app
